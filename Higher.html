HIGHER EDUCATION & RESEARCH

MPT provide a range of services that support teaching, learning, research,
and operational efficiency in higher education and research institutions.

Overview

Our Services

Overview

The higher education and research sector encompasses institutions and organizations
that provide post-secondary education, conduct research, and develop new
technologies. This sector includes:

  1. Universities and Colleges: Institutions that offer undergraduate and graduate degree programs.
  2. Research Institutes: Organizations that conduct research in various fields, such as science,
    technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).
  3. Libraries and Archives: Institutions that provide access to information resources, including books, joumals, and digital collections.

Services

MPT play a vital role in supporting the higher education and research sector by providing
a range of services, including:

  1. Learning Management Systems (LMS): Software platforms that enable online learning, course management, and student engagement.
  2. Research Computing: High-performance computing resources, such as clusters, grids, and clouds, that support research in various fields.
  3. Data Analytics: Services that help institutions analyze and interpret large datasets, including student data, research data, and operational data.
  4. Cybersecurity: Solutions that protect institutions from cyber threats, including network security, endpoint security, and incident response.
  5. Digital Libraries: Online platforms that provide access to digital collections, including e-books, journals, and other digital resources
  6. Collaboration Tools: Software platforms that enable collaboration among researchers, students, and faculty, including video conferencing, instant messaging, and project management
  7. Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML): Services that apply Al and ML to various applications, including student success, research, and operational efficiency.
  8. Cloud Services: Cloud-based infrastructure, platforms, and software that support teaching, learning, and research.
  9. IT Consulting: Services that help institutions assess, plan, and implement IT strategies, including technology roadmaps and IT governance.
  10. Digital Transformation: Services that help institutions transform their operations, including digitalization of administrative processes, online learning, and research support.
