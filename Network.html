Network Management

Here are some network management services provided by MPT

Our Service

Network Monitoring

  1. Real-time monitoring: Continuous monitoring of network performance, availability, and security.
  2. Alerts and notifications: Automated alerts and notifications for network issues, enabling swift action.

Network Maintenance

  1. Software updates and patches: Regular updates and patches for network devices and software.
  2. Configuration management: Management of network device configurations to ensure consistency and compliance.
  3. Backup and disaster recovery: Regular backups and disaster recovery planning to minimize downtime.

Network Security

  1. Functional Testing: Verifying that applications function as intended.
  2. Intrusion detection and prevention: Monitoring for potential security threats and prevention of unauthorized access.
  3. Vulnerability management: Identification and remediation of network vulnerabilities.

Network Performance Optimization

  1. Network traffic analysis: Analysis of network traffic to identify bottlenecks and optimize performance.
  2. Quality of Service (QoS): Configuration and management of QoS policies to prioritize critical applications.
  3. Capacity planning: Planning and provisioning of network resources to meet growing demands.

Network Troubleshooting

  1. Fault detection and diagnosis: Identification and diagnosis of network issues.
  2. Troubleshooting and repair: Swift troubleshooting and repair of network issues to minimize downtime.

Managed Network Services

  1. Managed Wi-Fi: Management of Wi-Fi networks, including configuration, security, and troubleshooting.
  2. Managed LAN/WAN: Management of local area networks (LANs) and wide area networks (WANs).
  3. Managed cloud services: Management of cloud-based services, including infrastructure, platforms, and applications.

Other Services

  1. Network design and implementation: Design and implementation of new networks or upgrades to existing networks.
  2. Network auditing and compliance: Auditing of networks to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards.
  3. Training and support: Training and support for network administrators and end-users.
